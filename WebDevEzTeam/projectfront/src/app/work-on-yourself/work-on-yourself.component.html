Exercises Categories:

Beginner - is the best option for those who has never been working out, or took a very long break since the last training. These workout exercises are simple, straightforward, injury-free, and fun to perform.

Continuing - is the option for those who already has some experience, started seeing positive changes in their body and health conditions, and it is also for those, who can do Beginner exercises without sweating, which by itself means that you are ready to challenge yourself more.

Advanced - these are the exercises that require skill, understanding, and developed muscle condition, so that injuries are avoided. This section is for those who’s already reached their limits, but are willing to push themselves even further. However, we highly recommend trying out our Beginner and Continuing sections first, to make sure that your workouts are as safe and productive as it is possible.
